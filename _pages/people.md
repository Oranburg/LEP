The substantive business law electives (Business Associations, Securities Regulation, M&A, Tax, Antitrust, Bankruptcy, Banking Law) are taught by CUA Law's existing faculty. The program does not duplicate or replace these courses. The full CUA Law faculty roster is at [law.edu](https://www.law.edu/).
