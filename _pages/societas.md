# Societas

The Latin word for partnership, drawn from Roman commercial law and developed by Aquinas in *Summa Theologiae* II-II q. 78 as the just form of capital deployment, in contrast to *mutuum* at interest. Maimonides reaches the same conclusion from the Jewish tradition: partnership (*shutafut*) is the highest of the eight rungs of *tzedakah*. This convergence is the program's intellectual center.
