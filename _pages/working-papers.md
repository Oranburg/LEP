# Working Papers

Drafts in development that grow out of the program's intellectual work. Working papers are not for citation without the author's permission.

## Current

### *Architects of the Void*

A law-review essay on the entrepreneurial lawyer as sub-creator. The argument identifies the lawyer's role in constituting the legal forms within which commercial life takes shape, and reads that role through the *societas* tradition rather than the gatekeeper tradition. *Status: in submission. Draft not circulated.*

### *In the Shadow of the Law*

A pedagogy essay on what changes in the law-school classroom when doctrine is taught as the byproduct of bargaining in the shadow of the law, rather than as the foreground of judicial opinion. *Status: in submission. Draft not circulated.*
