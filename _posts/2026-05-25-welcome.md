This site opens as a working space. It collects the doctrinal and intellectual frame of the law-and-entrepreneurship program in development at the Catholic University of America Columbus School of Law. It is not yet a CUA-authorized program site.

Content will accumulate as the program's intellectual identity matures: essays on the doctrinal lineage from *Rerum Novarum* to *Magnifica Humanitas*, on *societas* as the partnership form of justice, on the year-theme *Entrepreneurship v. Poverty*, and on the close reading of Pope Leo XIV's recent encyclical.

The program begins formally with Seth Oranburg's arrival at CUA Law on August 20, 2026.
