This site is the personal working space of the directors of the Law and Entrepreneurship Program at the Catholic University of America Columbus School of Law. The program is operating now under founding Director Alexander Hoffarth. Seth Oranburg joins as Director on August 20, 2026, holding the title of Ordinary Professor of Law and Director of the Law and Entrepreneurship Program.

The site collects the intellectual identity of the program in public: the doctrinal lineage from *Rerum Novarum* through *Magnifica Humanitas*, the *societas* tradition that lineage carries, the year-theme *Entrepreneurship v. Poverty*, and the reading of Pope Leo XIV's recent encyclical. It is not a CUA-authorized site; CUA-authorized program communications will appear on law.edu when the formal institutional process is complete.

For prospective and current students, the [For Students]({{ '/for-students/' | relative_url }}) area carries what you actually need: the prospective-student decision page, the year-by-year action page for current CUA students, the mentors-and-employers page, the full FAQ, and the glossary.
